Yes. Deactivate the old device in Settings → License, then activate your new machine. The Team plan covers 3 devices simultaneously — swap any of them at any time.
30 days, no questions asked. Email support@dockerman.app and we'll refund you in full. We'd rather have you come back later than feel stuck.
Genuinely free. No time limit, no feature throttling on local workflows. The paid tiers exist because remote hosts and multi-host management are significant engineering investments — and because we'd like to keep shipping.
If your Docker runs on the same machine as Dockerman, no. If you SSH into a NAS, a homelab server, a Raspberry Pi, or a cloud VM — you'll want Solo or Team. Most homelab folks go with Team because they have ≥2 machines already.
The paid plans (Solo and Team) include commercial use. For teams larger than 3, contact us at sales@dockerman.app for volume pricing. We offer PDF invoices, purchase orders, and W-9s on request.
Stripe secure checkout — all major credit cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and in supported countries: Alipay, WeChat Pay, SEPA, iDEAL. USD pricing; your bank handles the conversion.
Dockerman is local-first. It does not upload container data, images, logs, or command history anywhere. License validation happens once at activation and periodically (signed, no payload). Anonymous crash reports are opt-in.
